# Data-Fundamentals
All about SQL, MS-Excel, Python Pandas
A university collects academic performance data for students enrolled in different departments such as Computer Science and Information Technology. The dataset contains marks obtained by students in subjects like Mathematics, Science, and Programming along with attendance records.
The academic administration wants to evaluate student performance to understand learning outcomes and identify students who may require academic support.
The university wants to answer questions like:
Who are the top-performing students?
Which department performs better academically?
Are students with higher attendance performing better?
Which students are at risk due to low marks?
Students will analyze the dataset and generate insights to help the academic committee improve teaching strategies.
